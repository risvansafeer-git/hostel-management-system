Hostel Management System

Welcome to the Hostel Management System.

The Hostel Management System is a web-based application developed to simplify and automate hostel administration activities. Traditional hostel management often relies on manual records, making it difficult to manage student information, room allocation, complaints, leave requests, and notices efficiently.

Key Features
• Student Management
* Add, view, update, and delete student records.
* Manage hostel rooms and their capacities.
• Room Allocation
* Allocate rooms to students and maintain allocation records.
• Complaint Management
* Students can submit complaints and administrators can resolve them.
• Leave Management
* Students can apply for leave and administrators can approve or reject requests.
• Notice Board
* Administrators can publish important notices and announcements.
• Dashboard and Reports
* Provides summary statistics for effective hostel administration.
User Roles
Administrator:
* Full access to manage students, rooms, allocations, complaints, leave requests, and notices.
Student:
* Can view hostel information, notices, room allocation details, submit complaints, and apply for leave.
The objective of this project is to improve efficiency, reduce paperwork, enhance communication between hostel administrators and students, and provide a user-friendly hostel management solution.
