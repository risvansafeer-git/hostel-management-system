🏢 Hostel Management System

Welcome to Hostel Management System

The Hostel Management System is a web-based application developed to simplify hostel administration by automating student management, room allocation, complaint handling, leave management and notice publishing.

It provides a centralized platform that helps hostel administrators efficiently manage hostel activities while allowing students to access important hostel services online.

Key Features

👨‍🎓 Student Management
🏠 Room Management
🛏 Room Allocation
📝 Complaint Management
📅 Leave Management
📢 Notice Board
📊 Dashboard
🔐 Role Based Login

Administrator

Full access to manage students, rooms, room allocation, complaints, leave requests, notices and reports.

Student

Can view hostel details, notices, allocated rooms, submit complaints, and apply for leave.
